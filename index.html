Core Features

Comprehensive Safety Ecosystem

Our integrated platform combines cutting-edge technologies to create an unparalleled tourist safety monitoring and response system.

Digital ID Platform

Secure, blockchain-based digital identity with KYC, itinerary, and emergency contacts, valid only for the trip duration.

Smart Mobile App

AI-powered app with safety scores, geo-fencing alerts, a one-touch panic button, and opt-in tracking for family.

AI Anomaly Detection

Platform Pillars

Security & Accessibility

Data Privacy & Security

Our system is built on a foundation of trust. We employ end-to-end encryption for all communications and leverage blockchain for tamper-proof identity records, ensuring full compliance with data protection laws.

Multilingual Support

To ensure accessibility for all visitors, the TouristGuard mobile app and platform are available in English and 10+ major Indian languages, with voice and text-based emergency access for all users.

How It Works

A Streamlined Process Flow

From arrival to departure, our system provides a seamless safety net with four simple and effective steps.

Digital ID Registration

Upon arrival, tourists complete a quick, secure KYC process using their Aadhaar or passport at a designated kiosk.

  • Biometric verification
  • Trip itinerary recording
  • Emergency contacts added

Mobile App Setup

Tourists download the TouristGuard app, which syncs with their new Digital ID to activate safety features.

  • App installation & sync
  • Geo-fence preferences set
  • Safety score calibrated

Real-time Monitoring

Our AI continuously and non-intrusively monitors location data for anomalies against their planned itinerary.

  • Passive location tracking
  • Intelligent pattern analysis
  • Predictive risk assessment

Alert & Response

If an anomaly or panic signal is detected, instant alerts are sent to the command center and emergency contacts.

  • Automated authority notification
  • Emergency contact updates
  • Incident documentation begins
Command Center

Real-time Dashboard

A comprehensive control center providing tourism departments and law enforcement with real-time insights and emergency response capabilities.

Heat Map Analysis

Visual representation of tourist density and dynamic risk zones with real-time updates for proactive policing.

Digital ID Records

Secure access to tourist profiles, travel history, itinerary, and emergency contacts for swift verification.

Auto E-FIR

Automated generation of a First Information Report (E-FIR) when a tourist is flagged as missing after prolonged anomaly.
